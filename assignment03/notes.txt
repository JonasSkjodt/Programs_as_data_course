Example from 3.6.6
Main ::= Expr EOF rule A
Expr ::= NAME rule B
    | CSTINT rule C
    | MINUS CSTINT rule D
    | LPAR Expr RPAR rule E
    | LET NAME EQ Expr IN Expr END rule F
    | Expr TIMES Expr rule G
    | Expr PLUS Expr rule H
    | Expr MINUS Expr rule I
